Trang Dang

“7 years” is neither too long nor too short that I spent doing Marketing and Branding at the foreign-based organizations and I was playing mid-management role for 5 years. At that time, I usually worked with the designers for my campaigns’ visuals and I gradually realized that I have a penchant for creative work, especially for aesthetic taste.

So I decided to take 8 months off from work to discover my artistic potential by enrolling myself in the Diploma of Multimedia, including Graphic Design Semester and UX/UI Semester, at Arena Multimedia school operated by Aptech International.

Fortunately, I received many appraisals from my instructors and my classmates for my designs and creative works since I know how to balance my insightful expertise in Branding & Marketing and my aesthetic creativity to generate ideas. This is also proven through my acedemic results, I get perfect marks over 90% of subjects.

And currently, I decided to take on a visual creative career as a Graphic Designer. I believe that pursuing my dream career at this age is not a fantasy, it is my courage and endless effort. I can utilize my strong understanding and expertise in Brand Communications to deliver not only creative but insightful designs that could meet business’ needs better.
